Reviews

  • The Phantom Rocks Opera!

    The digital book and movie are excellent quality! I always loved Webber's The Phantom of the Opera, but I hadn't read the book until now. Leroux's writing style is witty despite the serious subject. It's interesting to see a lawyer's take on the gothic horror genre. The novel is an excellent addition to your POTO collection. Purchase this set. You won't be disappointed!

  • Katniss Everdeen is a Strong Female Role Model!

    The Hunger Games trilogy provides a strong female character in Katniss Everdeen, brought to life in the films by Jennifer Lawrence. The Hunger Games will make you cheer and cry for the protagonist with its edge of your seat, thrills and heartbreakingly, beautiful moments. Purchase this set. And remember, "May the odds be forever in your favor."

  • Frankenstein is not the Monster!

    Who would have thought a person's dream could inspire this masterpiece. Mary Shelley wrote Frankenstein after a nightmare she had about a creature. The rest, as they say, is history. The novel is a classic for a reason. Antiheroes and empathetic characters draw you into the plot, and you will be glued to your seat until you finish it. The film directed by Kevin Connor, is a sensitive portrait of the monster who longed to be a man. I can't encourage you enough to purchase this duo.
