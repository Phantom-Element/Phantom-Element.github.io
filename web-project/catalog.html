Catalog

The Doors/No One Here Gets Out Alive

The biography and accompanying biopic about lead singer of The Doors, Jim Morrison. No One Here Gets Out Alive, takes you on a journey of the rock star's tragic life and death. This is a set that is not to be missed.

DVD/Paperback Combo - $25


Frankenstein

Mary Shelley's Frankenstein is a gothic classic that all are familiar with but may not have read. Some may think of the monster strapped to a table and being brought to life by lightning. The novel digs deeper than the classic monster movie and provides commentary on society and what it means to be human. The accompanying film directed by Kevin Connor and starring Donald Sutherland provides an astounding visual of this classic tale.

DVD/Paperback Combo - $30


The Hunger Games

Katniss Everdeen lives in a dystopian society where all children from the ages of 12 to 18 must play in a life or death match called The Hunger Games. Join Katniss in her heroic journey as she fights for herself and the people she loves. Read the novel that started it all, written by Suzanne Collins.

DVD/Paperback Combo - $23


The Phantom of the Opera

Gaston Leroux's The Phantom of the Opera inspired sequels, movies, and the famous broadway musical produced by Andrew Lloyd Webber. Another tragic but beautiful story about a masked monster hated by society; and the salvation he found within his love for an opera singer. Now you can watch Andrew Lloyd Webber's film version of his musical, starring Gerard Butler and Emmy Rossum.

DVD/Paperback Combo - $26
